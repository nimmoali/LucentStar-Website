Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Applies to: LucentSignal application and lucentstar.ai

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules governing your use of the LucentSignal platform and the lucentstar.ai website, operated by LucentStar AI. This AUP forms part of our Terms of Service. By using the Service, you agree to comply with this policy.

1. Purpose

LucentSignal is designed to help B2B marketing professionals create high-quality, thoughtful content. This policy exists to protect users, third parties, and the integrity of our platform. Misuse of AI content generation tools carries reputational and legal risks — for users and for us. We take this seriously.

2. Prohibited Content

You must not use LucentSignal to generate, store, schedule, or distribute content that:

2.1 Unlawful content

Violates any applicable law or regulation in the UK, EU, or any other jurisdiction

Constitutes or encourages criminal activity

Infringes the intellectual property rights of any person or organisation

Breaches data protection law or discloses personal data without a lawful basis

2.2 Harmful or abusive content

Is defamatory, threatening, harassing, or abusive

Promotes or incites hatred, discrimination, or violence against any group

Sexualises minors or promotes exploitation

Constitutes non-consensual intimate imagery or content

2.3 Deceptive and misleading content

Intentionally misleads or deceives readers

Impersonates any person, brand, or organisation without their authorisation

Makes false claims about products, services, or individuals

Is designed to spread misinformation or disinformation

Purports to be authored by a human when it is AI-generated, in contexts where disclosure is required by law or platform policy

2.4 Spam and unsolicited communications

Constitutes unsolicited commercial communications (spam) in violation of PECR or equivalent laws

Is designed for mass distribution without appropriate consent

Is used to manipulate platform algorithms or engagement metrics artificially

3. Prohibited Activities

You must not use LucentSignal or lucentstar.ai to:

Circumvent, disable, or interfere with security features, access controls, or usage quotas

Access or attempt to access any account or system other than your own

Reverse-engineer, decompile, or extract the underlying AI model, system prompts, or scoring algorithms

Use automated tools, bots, or scripts to interact with the Service beyond normal individual use

Resell, white-label, or redistribute the Service or its outputs commercially without a separate written agreement with LucentStar AI

Share your account credentials with third parties

Create multiple accounts to circumvent usage limits or plan restrictions

Conduct load testing, penetration testing, or vulnerability scanning without our prior written consent

Use the Service in any way that could damage, overload, or impair our infrastructure or affect other users

4. AI-Specific Requirements

Given that LucentSignal generates content using AI, you additionally agree to:

Review all AI-generated content before publishing or distributing it

Not rely solely on AI-generated content for decisions with significant legal, financial, or reputational consequences

Comply with the AI content disclosure policies of any platform on which you publish content (including LinkedIn's creator policies)

Not use the Service to generate content designed to circumvent AI detection systems where disclosure of AI generation is legally required

Take personal responsibility for the accuracy and appropriateness of all content you publish, whether AI-generated or otherwise

5. Reporting a Violation

If you believe another user is violating this policy, or if you encounter content generated using LucentSignal that concerns you, please contact us at hello@lucentstar.ai with as much detail as possible.

6. Consequences of Breach

We reserve the right to take the following actions in response to violations of this policy:

Issue a warning and require removal of offending content

Suspend access to the Service temporarily

Terminate your account without refund

Report unlawful activity to relevant authorities

We will use reasonable judgement in determining appropriate responses and will consider the severity, intent, and history of any breach.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated to registered users by email with at least 14 days notice. Continued use of the Service after the notice period constitutes acceptance. Last updated: 6 May 2026.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@lucentstar.ai
